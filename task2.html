Concept

From a starter demo to a full parallax experience.

The original sample used three background layers and a few rocks. This version expands that idea into a complete atmosphere: deep sky gradients, clustered star fields, soft nebula clouds, multiple terrain silhouettes, and ambient motion that reacts to scroll position.

What Changed

More depth

Foreground mist, three mountain ranges, dual star fields, and separate celestial objects create stronger visual separation.

Better structure

The page is now a complete, deployable HTML document instead of a fragment, with clearer content sections and calls to action.

Modern motion

Parallax movement is handled with requestAnimationFrame, CSS variables, and reduced-motion support rather than jQuery top offsets.

Design Notes

Intentional contrast, layered silhouettes, and motion restraint.

The visual direction leans cinematic rather than generic: warm gold highlights, deep navy shadows, angular mountain cuts, and fog that softens the lower frame. Faster elements sit closer to the viewer while the distant sky barely shifts, which keeps the effect readable instead of chaotic.
